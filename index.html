Business & Services

Everything interiors, with exterior craft to match.

Inner Living Interiors has been delivering exceptional renovations and interior solutions for over 25 years. Specialising in renovations, project management, steel fabrication, soft furnishings, and interior design, we create stylish, functional spaces with quality craftsmanship and expert attention to detail.

Interior design

  • Design consultations and colour selections
  • 2D and 3D renders, mood boards and space planning
  • Wallpapers for all colour palettes, rooms and moods
  • Design styles from geometric and textured to floral and botanicals

Soft furnishings

  • Curtain manufacture, roller blinds and window treatments
  • Upholstery, cushions and high-end fabric sourcing
  • Custom headboards, chairs, ottomans and lounge suites

Exterior fabrication

  • Customised railings in steel, timber, composite and tension cable
  • Stainless steel railings available too
  • Gates, burglar bars, secure enclosures and steel frames
  • Doors, windows, pergolas, decking, structural steel and timber works
